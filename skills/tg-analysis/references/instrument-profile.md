# TG Instrument Profile

TG/TGA measures sample mass as temperature or time increases under a controlled atmosphere.

## What the signal can support

- Total mass loss
- Number of apparent decomposition stages
- Approximate onset, peak, and end temperatures of each stage
- Final residue or ash fraction
- Presence of low-temperature volatile loss

## What the signal cannot support alone

- Exact chemical species identity
- Crystalline phase identification
- Definitive atmosphere reaction mechanism
- Exact composition fractions without calibration or orthogonal data

## Common atmospheres and caveats

- `N2` or `Ar`: emphasizes pyrolysis and volatilization
- `Air` or `O2`: adds oxidation and burnout stages
- Unknown atmosphere: interpretations must stay conservative
