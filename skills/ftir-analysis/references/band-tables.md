# Common FTIR Band Anchors

These anchors are screening references only. Match them conservatively and in context.

## Functional-group region

- `3700-3200 cm^-1`: O-H stretch, N-H stretch, free or hydrogen-bonded hydroxyl bands
- `3100-3000 cm^-1`: aromatic or unsaturated C-H stretch
- `2960-2850 cm^-1`: aliphatic C-H stretch
- `1750-1700 cm^-1`: carbonyl-rich region
- `1650-1600 cm^-1`: H-O-H bending, aromatic C=C, amide-related modes, or other context-dependent bands

## Fingerprint region

- `1250-1000 cm^-1`: C-O, Si-O, P-O, S-O, and related skeletal vibrations
- `900-700 cm^-1`: out-of-plane bending or framework vibrations
- `600-400 cm^-1`: metal-oxygen or lattice-like vibrations in many inorganic systems

## Current sample anchors

- `3431.23 cm^-1`: broad O-H / N-H-like screening band
- `1625.22 cm^-1`: weak water-bending-like or other context-dependent band
- `1109.35 cm^-1`: strongest fingerprint-region band
- `802.72 cm^-1`: secondary fingerprint-region band
- `473.92 cm^-1`: low-wavenumber lattice-like band
