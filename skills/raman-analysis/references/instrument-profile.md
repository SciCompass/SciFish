## What the spectrum can support

- dominant band position extraction
- relative intensity ranking
- publication-style spectrum plotting
- conservative pattern comments on band sharpness and band families
- tentative carbon-like screening when D/G/2D-style anchors are present

## What the spectrum cannot support alone

- definitive material identification
- laser-dependent interpretation details
- quantitative defect density or crystallite size
- composition confirmation without external references

## Writing discipline

- Treat the measured spectrum as the primary evidence.
- Separate observations from interpretations.
- State when band assignments are tentative.
